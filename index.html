Our Excellent Services

A responsive, secure online police complaint service lets citizens report incidents, upload evidence, and track case progress entirely online; it provides guided complaint categories, optional anonymous filing, automatic routing to the correct police unit, real-time status updates via SMS and email, and a clear pathway to convert a submission into a formal FIR when required. The service includes multilingual support, mobile-friendly access, two-way secure messaging with assigned officers, appointment booking for verifications, and strong privacy controls with encrypted data storage and audit logs to protect chain of custody. For administrators, it offers triage rules, officer dashboards, case management tools, and analytics to monitor response times and outcomes, improving transparency, accountability, and citizen satisfaction.

Mobile theft

Report a stolen or lost mobile quickly through this portal to start an official investigation and protect your device. Provide incident details (date, time, location, description of circumstances), device information (brand, model, IMEI/MEID number, colour, distinguishing marks), SIM details (mobile number and last seen status), and any witness or CCTV leads; attach photos, receipts, or screenshots as evidence.

harrasments/abusements

Report incidents of harassment or abuse through this portal to initiate prompt police action and access support services. Provide incident details including date, time, location, description of what happened, names of the accused if known, and any witnesses or prior incidents. Attach evidence such as screenshots, chat logs, call records, photos, or video clips and indicate whether you wish to file the complaint anonymously or with contact details for follow-up.

Police Complaint Registration

Use this form to report incidents online. Fill required fields and attach any supporting evidence.

Choose anonymous to hide your contact details from public summaries; police may need verification for FIRs.

You can add landmark or exact address to help local station assignment.

Max 5 files, 20 MB each. Do not upload sensitive personal documents unless necessary.

I declare that the information provided is true to the best of my knowledge and consent to its use for investigation. False reporting is subject to legal action.
After submission

You will receive a Case ID by SMS or email. Expect initial acknowledgement within the stated SLA and messages from the assigned station.

Emergency

If you are in immediate danger call local emergency number instead of using the online form.
